Supermodel

Democratizing the power of machine learning.

Supermodel is a web application that allows for mainstream use of machine learning. By ingesting custom data sets, Supermodel can provide hypertuned models that are optimal for that data set. By taking the rigor out of machine learning, Supermodel aims to ease the tasks of analyzing data and acting on data.

Bipartisan

Combating fake news, one article at a time.

Conceived at HopHacks @ Johns Hopkins University, Bipartisan seeks to rate news articles by credibility and also serve as a hub that provides reliable news stories. I worked on the Natural Language Processing model that extracted features from articles, scripts that streamed in thousands of articles a minute, and a bit on the Random Forest classifier.
